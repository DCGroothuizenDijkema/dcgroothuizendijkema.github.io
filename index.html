David C Groothuizen Dijkema

I am a doctoral candidate in mathematics at the University of Auckland, under the supervision of Claire Postlethwaite and Vivien Kirk. I am interested in the mathematical theory of dynamical systems, and my thesis is about dynamics near structures known as heteroclinic networks.
